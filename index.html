📖 Shadows to Sunlight: The 90-Day Rise

Chapter 1: The Golden Bond & The Bitter Betrayal

Anya and Aliza were the most famous best friends in the academy. Anya trusted Aliza blindly. She shared every little secret, dream, and weakness with her. But Aliza was secretly jealous. One day, after a small fight, Aliza broke Anya's trust completely. She told all of Anya’s personal secrets to the whole academy and shared them on social media. Anya was shocked and deeply hurt. Everyone started talking behind her back. Anya became completely alone, cried every day, and lost all interest in her studies.

Chapter 2: The New Light & The Silent Pact

While Anya was sad and sitting alone, a girl named Meerab noticed her. Meerab was a very kind, honest, and smart student. She sat next to Anya, wiped her tears, and said, "Crying will only make your enemies happy. If you want revenge, take it with your success." Meerab's friendship gave Anya a new hope. Together, they made a silent promise: they would disappear from social media and from the world for the next 90 days, focusing purely on hard work and self-care.

Chapter 3: The Ultimate 90-Day Transformation

These 90 days were the hardest but most powerful days of Anya’s life. She deleted all distractions and woke up early every morning to study with laser-sharp focus. Whenever she struggled with hard physics or chemistry concepts, Meerab was always there to help her. Along with studying, Anya started using natural kitchen remedies for her skin and health. Soon, her face got a healthy, natural glow, and her confidence returned. This was true friendship, where there was no jealousy, only support.

Chapter 4: The Result & The Final Justice

When the board exam results came after 90 days, everyone in the academy was shocked. Anya got the **First Position** in the entire district! Her face was bright with success, and her whole world changed. All the students who used to ignore her now wanted to be her friend. Meerab stood proudly by her side, smiling. On the other hand, Aliza was left completely alone. When people realized that Aliza was a fake friend who could not keep secrets, everyone stopped talking to her. Aliza was left crying in loneliness, while Anya walked into a bright future with her true best friend.
